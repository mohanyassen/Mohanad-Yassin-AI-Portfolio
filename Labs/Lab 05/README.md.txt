# L05 — RNN and Transformers

## Overview

This lab explores Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs) and Transformer architectures used in modern artificial intelligence and Natural Language Processing systems.

The lab focuses on how sequential data is processed and how transformer models improve language understanding and AI performance.

---

# Objectives

* Understand how RNNs process sequential data
* Explore the limitations of traditional recurrent networks
* Learn the fundamentals of Transformer architectures
* Compare RNNs and Transformers in AI applications
* Analyze modern NLP model workflows

---

* Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs)
* Sequential Data Processing
